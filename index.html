A Few Words About Us

StoneGuard is a leading provider of comprehensive data protection services, dedicated to helping organizations navigate the complexities of data privacy regulations. Our mission is to empower Data Controllers by offering expert guidance, tailored assessments, and effective solutions to ensure compliance and build trust.

Our Core Services

1. CDPA Gap Analysis

  • Comprehensive assessments to identify compliance gaps relative to the Zimbabwean Cybersecurity and Data Protection Act (CDPA).
  • Evaluation of existing policies and practices against regulatory requirements.
  • Actionable recommendations for compliance improvement.

2. CDPA Compliance

  • Support in implementing compliant policies and procedures
  • Training sessions for staff on CDPA requirements and best practices.
  • Development of essential compliance documentation.

3. DPO Services

  • Part-time DPO services available for half a day each week over a two-week period.
  • Ongoing support for compliance monitoring and guidance.
  • Assistance with incident response and data breach management.

1. Additional Data Protection Services:

  1. CDPA Audit: In-depth audits to ensure adherence to CDPA standards.
  2. Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA): Assisting in identifying and mitigating risks related to data processing activities.
  3. CDPA Consultancy: Expert consultation services tailored to your organization's specific needs.
  4. Other Privacy Impact Assessments: Support for various privacy impact assessments tailored to specific projects or regulations.
  5. Data Breach Mitigation: Strategies and support to manage data breaches effectively.
  6. Annual CDPA Review: Regular reviews to ensure ongoing compliance and effectiveness of data protection measures.
  7. Records of Processing Activities (ROPA): Assistance in maintaining accurate records of data processing activities
  8. CDPA Data Retention: Guidance on data retention policies to comply with legal requirements.
  9. Data Subject Access Requests (DSAR): Support in managing and responding to requests from data subjects.

2. Specialized:

  1. Mergers and Acquisitions: Data protection assessments and compliance strategies for companies involved in M&A.
  2. Third Party/Processors: Evaluation and management of third-party data processing agreements.

3. International services:

  1. Data Outside Zimbabwe: Expertise in managing data protection compliance for international data transfers.
  2. Representation for International Companies: Serving as a local representative for foreign organizations navigating Zimbabwean data protection laws..
